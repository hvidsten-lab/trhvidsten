Conclusion: Our approach performs on par or better than the previously published analyses when it comes to extracting significant binding site modules. Furthermore, since our modules are smaller (contain fewer genes), a larger fraction of the genes need to be co-annotated or bound by the same transcription factors than in the previous studies to yield the same P-values. The observed fraction of pairs of genes with the same annotation/transcription factor binding clearly shows a stronger coherence in modules discovered in the current study.
